Hangman is a classic word guessing game. The goal of the game is to guess a secret word by guessing one letter at a time. For each incorrect guess, a part of a hanged man is drawn. If the entire man is drawn before the secret word is guessed, the player loses. If the secret word is guessed before the man is complete, the player wins.

How to Play?

1. The game starts with a blank gallows and a row of dashes representing the secret word. 2. The player guesses a letter. 3. If the letter is in the secret word, the letter is revealed in the correct position. 4. If the letter is not in the secret word, a part of the hanged man is drawn. 5. The player continues guessing letters until the secret word is guessed or the man is complete.
